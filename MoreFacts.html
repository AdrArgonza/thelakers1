More Fun Facts

  • The team has won 16 titles in all—the second highest number in the NBA.
    • <
  • Thanks to Magic Johnson, the Lakers invented a new style of gameplay called “showtime”: it consisted of fastbreak scoring and a flashy offense.

  • In all of NBA’s extensive history, the Lakers hold the title for the longest winning streak. In their 1971-1972 seasons, the team won an astonishing 33 games in a row.

  • Over time, the Lakers have produced a large number of notable players who achieved greatness with induction into the Basketball Hall of Fame. The most notable of these great players are Abdul-Jabbar, Wilt Chamberlain, Karl Malone, Kobe Bryant, and of course Magic Johnson.

  • The Lakers became such a famous that the team has the highest amount of Facebook followers than any other team in the U.S. sports industry.

  • Before moving to the Staples Centre in 1999, the 'Great Western Forum' was where the Lakers played against their opponents.
